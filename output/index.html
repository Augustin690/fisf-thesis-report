Patterns

Key Insights

MFin Concentration

Teaching-track faculty Tang Dunzhe (73 MFin) and Tong Guoshi (62 MFin) supervise the most MFin theses, accounting for 26% of all 512 MFin theses between just two supervisors.

Degree Split

Of 1,194 total theses, 512 are MFin (43%), 487 are MBA (41%), and 195 are EMBA (16%). Tenure-track faculty supervise across all three; practice-track and external faculty lean heavily toward MBA/EMBA.

100% MFin

John Rogers supervised exclusively MFin students (21/21), reflecting his role as a dedicated research mentor for the Master of Finance program before departing FISF. Fu Chengde (7/7) also supervises only MFin.

Faculty Turnover

Several supervisors have left FISF: Zhou Yifan (33 theses, now at XJTLU), Nedopil Wang (21, Griffith), Zhou Sili (19, Macau), Guo Xiangyu (9, Tsinghua), Rogers and Peng Qiusha. Yi Huang is on leave.

Research Breadth

FISF's 36 supervisors span a wide research spectrum: from macro/international finance (Ma, Wu, Rogers, Jiao, Huang) to asset pricing (Tong, Sun, Chen H., Lu, Zhou S.), corporate finance (Gao, Qian, Zhou Y.), FinTech (Tang D., Chang, Xu), econometrics (Jiang, Tang H., Fuh), green finance (Nedopil, Chen S., Li Z.), and accounting (Shi W., Zhu). PhDs come from Chicago, Columbia, Cambridge, Berkeley, UPenn, JHU, LBS, INSEAD, Oxford, and other top institutions worldwide.
